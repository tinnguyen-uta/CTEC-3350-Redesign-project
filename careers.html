Careers

Are you an entertainer or artist wishing to be represented by a company? Do you have a passion for making people laugh, smile and feel joy? Do you want to work an exciting and rewarding job that always involves having a great time? B3 may have a job for you! Here at B3 Entertainment, we as a team value good communication, a positive attitude and fun! If you possess these qualities, we may have a job for you.

Our Values

Communication

As they say, communication is key! This is a very important value, as it sets up the foundation for all our work from the beginning. As a team, we must stay on the same page with eachother and our clients.

Positivity

In order to bring the best experience to our customers, we have to be positive! In any moment things can get very busy, that's where your attitude comes in! Staying optimistic under pressure is a valuable trait in all of our teammates!

Fun

Last but not least: Fun. This is what our company is all about! Fun is our initial goal and end result for any and every even that we take part in. Not only should our events be enjoyable for our guests, but for us too! It makes the work not even feel like work!
