ABOUT

Hello, Welcome! We are Lauren and Paul Brooks, founders of Dallas based B3 Entertainment Productions, Inc. This company is our passion, as it brings joy, fun, and excitement to others as well as ourselves! We love working with entertainers, artists, and other companies to provide an entertaining and memorable experience for everybody involved! See what we're all about by contacting us and exploring our services page!

If you want to contact us for more information, email: info@b3talent.com

Our hours are Monday-Friday 10am-5pm.
